About Slope

Slope Unblocked is a game that was developed by RobKay in 2014. It quickly gained immense popularity among players in the USA, Canada, UK, and Australia. Let's try to figure out why Slope became so popular.

Slope Unblocked is a game in the endless arcade genre. This means that the game has no end, and you can play it for as long as you have the time. You are in control of a huge green ball that constantly falls down a slope in a fictional city, which is entirely composed of skyscrapers and neon lights in green color. The player must be highly focused because there will be many dangerous and tricky obstacles in their way. These could include narrow tunnels, moving walls, tight paths, and bottomless pits. The game is controlled with the keyboard using the WASD keys or arrow keys. In Slope unblocked game, you'll also find a leaderboard that is updated every day. Only the champions make it there! Try to score as many points as possible and reach the number 1 spot. We wish you good luck and a great game!
